This article compares eSIM and iSIM technologies for IoT devices, explaining that eSIM solders a reprogrammable chip directly to the circuit board while iSIM embeds SIM functionality into the main processor itself. The guide concludes that eSIM suits industrial and ruggedized applications with mature ecosystem support, whereas iSIM offers advantages for ultra-compact devices with better power efficiency and lower costs at high volumes.
